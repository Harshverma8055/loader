Understanding Tractor HP and Front Loader Stress

Tractor HP dictates the volume and pressure capacity of the hydraulic pump. Running a loader larger than the tractor HP rating can overload the front axle spindles and bypass valves. Madina Engineering designs custom brackets to distribute heavy loader forces along the full length of the tractor chassis back to the rear axle housing.

All our loaders feature subframe mounts that run under the driver footplate, preventing shear stress at the mid-engine split seam casing. We secure these with high-tensile 8.8-grade hardened steel fasteners.
