Yes, our loaders are engineered to be universally compatible with almost all Indian tractors above 35 HP. We design customized side support bracket plates for Swaraj, John Deere, Mahindra, Massey Ferguson, Sonalika, New Holland, etc., to match their chassis mounting holes exactly.
Our Standard Loader model goes up to 12 Feet (lift height) with a 1.5-ton capacity. The Heavy-Duty Loader model reaches up to 14 Feet (tipper height) with a tested 2.0-ton capacity. The actual lifting capacity also depends on the hydraulic oil pressure of your tractor.
We fabricate the brackets in our factory block according to your tractor model measurements. We can send our technical installation team to fit the loader directly at your site, or you can bring your tractor to our Mehandwas workshop in Tonk for mounting.
We offer a 1-year structural warranty on our main loader arms and bucket structures, and 6 months replacement coverage on hydraulic cylinder seals and valve distributors against manufacturing defects.

🛡️ Warranty Coverage Policy Summary

At Madina Engineering Works, we stand behind our workmanship. Each loader comes with a printed warranty booklet stamped at the time of delivery or showroom installation. Here is what is covered:

Loader Arms & Subframe

12 Months Coverage: Free structural repair or replacement if cracks or structural welds fail under normal operating loads.

Hydraulic Cylinders

6 Months Coverage: Replacement of internal gland oil seals, rod wiper packings, and piston valves in case of leakage.

Distributor Blocks & Hoses

6 Months Coverage: Replacement coverage against manufacturing defects in the hand spool control lever blocks.
