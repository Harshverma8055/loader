Yes, our loaders are engineered to be universally compatible with almost all Indian tractors. We design customized side support bracket plates for Swaraj, John Deere, Mahindra, Massey Ferguson, Sonalika, New Holland, etc., to match their chassis mounting holes exactly.
Our Standard Loader model goes up to 14 Feet (lift height) with a 1.5-ton capacity. The Telescopic Loader model reaches up to 24 Feet (tipper height) with a tested 1.5-ton capacity. The actual lifting capacity also depends on the hydraulic oil pressure of your tractor.
We assemble the brackets in our factory block according to your tractor model measurements. We can send our technical installation team to fit the loader directly at your site, or you can bring your tractor to our Mehandwas workshop in Tonk for mounting.
We offer a warranty of 6 months or 600 hours (whichever comes first) on the machine, and 3 months or 300 hours for hydraulic seals. The warranty is valid only if the machine has not been repaired, modified, or tampered with by any unauthorized person. Any work performed by a third party will void the warranty.
