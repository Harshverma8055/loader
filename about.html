Our Story

18+ Years of Industrial Engineering

From a regional fabrication workshop in 2008 to India's trusted manufacturer of heavy-duty tractor loaders and agricultural attachments.

Fabrication Ethics

State-of-the-Art Manufacturing Facility

Our plant in Mehandwas, Tonk operates precision lathe machinery, heavy sheet bending presses, and dedicated CO2 MIG welding bays to construct loader frames capable of lifting 2.2 Tons safely.

  • ⚡

    CO2 MIG Welding: Offers superior joint fusion and deep steel penetration compared to traditional arc welding, preventing arm frame structural failure under tension.

  • ⚡

    Telescopic Lathe Turning: Shapes solid steel hinge pins and internal cylinder rods within micron-level tolerances to guarantee leak-free rotations.

  • ⚡

    Chemical Derusting Bath: Every completed loader undergoes chemical priming baths to remove structural scale before entering double-coat baking enamel cabins.

Evolution

Timeline of Our Growth

Key milestones representing our commitment to supporting Indian agricultural and construction businesses.

2008

Foundation

Madina Engineering Works was established as a localized custom agricultural repair shop and iron plow manufacturer in Mehandwas, Rajasthan.

2013

First Hydraulic Prototypes

Engineered and tested our first tractor-mounted front-end hydraulic loader designed for regional sand mines and brick kiln fuel handling.

2018

Expanding the Catalog

Introduced custom-mounting loader brackets compatible with multiple tractor brands (Swaraj, Mahindra, John Deere) and launched the specialized sugarcane grabber.

2026

Nationwide Fleet Presence

Over 500 loaders deployed across brick kilns, farm fields, and warehouse hubs in Rajasthan, MP, Haryana, and UP.
